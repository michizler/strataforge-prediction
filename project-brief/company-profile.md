# Company Profile  
## StrataForge Construction Materials Ltd.  
### “Engineering Strength from the Ground Up”

---

## 1. Company Overview

StrataForge Construction Materials Ltd. is a mid-sized UK-based construction and materials engineering firm specialising in commercial foundations, high-load structural slabs, transport infrastructure, and precast concrete solutions.

For over 18 years, StrataForge has built its reputation on delivering durable, specification-compliant concrete for:

- High-rise developments  
- Industrial warehouses  
- Bridge decks and reinforced transport corridors  
- Renewable energy foundations  

However, as project complexity increased and margins tightened, one recurring issue began to threaten operational efficiency:

> **Inconsistent compressive strength outcomes across different concrete mixes.**
